📱 Check an Email or Text for Fraud

First: To check an email or text message on your phone to see if it is potentially fraudulent, first take a screen shot of the message by following the instructions below.

  • iPhone: Press Side Button + Volume Up at the same time
  • Android: Press Power Button + Volume Down at the same time

Next: The screenshot will save to your Photos. Use the Select Screenshot button below to select it for fraud assessment.
